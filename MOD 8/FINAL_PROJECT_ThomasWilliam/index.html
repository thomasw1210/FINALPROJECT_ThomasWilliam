Who Are We?

SodaStream was dreamed up as the brainchild of an avid soda-enthusiast, and amateur gamer. The central idea behind our business is Fun in all it's Forms. All of our team members and affiliates share in the vision of creating the best yet combination of a soda bar, digital recreation facility, and online gaming streaming forum. We aim to provide a fun and familiar environment for gaming and soda enthusiasts alike!

What do We offer?

As our name suggests, SodaStream is serious about soda! We maintain a fully stocked soft-drink fountain including all the local favorites, plus some flavors from all over the world - not to mention the endless possibilities offered through our 'make-your-own-flavor' Italian Soda bar! And while you enjoy your ice cold custom carbonated treat, you can kick back in our lounge and enjoy free WiFi, live streaming video games, or try your hand at topping the leaderboards on our formidible array of gaming consoles and computers in the GamerZone!
